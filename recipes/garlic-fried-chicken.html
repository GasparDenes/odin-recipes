Garlic Fried Chicken

Home

Description

Crispy, golden chicken pieces are coated in a flavorful garlic-infused batter and fried to perfection. Each bite delivers a savory and aromatic crunch, with just the right balance of garlic and seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
